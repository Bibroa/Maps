Skills2Capabilities - Regional Maps Overview

This page provides interactive visualisations developed within the framework of the Skills2Capabilities project. The maps illustrate regional economic, social, digital, and environmental characteristics across selected countries participating in the Project.

Available Maps

  • Economic Patents (Germany & Austria)
  • Median Age by Region
  • Net Migration by Region
  • Population Aged 65 and Over
  • Broadband Coverage by Region
  • Regional Share of Industrial Employment
  • Regional Unemployment Rates
  • Just Transition Fund Eligibility
  • Share of Renewable Energy

Data sources: Statistics Italy (ISTAT), Office for National Statistics (UK), BBSR (Germany), Statistics Finland (Tilastokeskus), Statistics Austria (Statistik Austria), Statistics Estonia (Eesti Statistika), Statistics Netherlands (CBS).
